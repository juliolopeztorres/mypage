Welcome to my personal page on GitHub

Julio is currently working as developer in Creative Quality S.L. building web, mobile and desktop apps. He studied successfully Telecommunication Engineer during 2009 - 2014 in Universidad de Granada.

His final degree project was about the development of a compact and portable prototype for the determination of gaseous oxyxen in breath. The prototype is based on an optical luminescent sensor consisting of a stabilized porphyrin dye directly deposited onto a color detector.

For this particular optical sensor, the red coordinate is directly related to the concentration of O2, and it is sent to a remote device such as a smartphone or tablet by a Bluetooth link. An application for Android operative system has also been developed for data visualization and analysis.

With the help of his teachers, it was possible to publish this work on IEEE Sensors Journal (Check it out). After his studies, he focus on learning web developing and improving his skills in current programming languages as PHP, HTML, JavaScript and the main frameworks for these languages (i.e. Laravel, Smarty, Angular JS, Ember JS, JQuery, Bootstrap or Foundation).

As a passionate of the technology and IT, he is always learning and leveling up his skills.
